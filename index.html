Design Direction

How the New Site Should Look & Feel

Four possible approaches. We recommend Heritage Premium — it leans into Parkstone's unique 100+ year story while feeling contemporary.

Heritage Premium

Navy, cream, burnt orange, gold. Traditional and trustworthy with modern execution. Leans into 100+ years of history. This is our recommendation.

Coastal Modern

Teal, white, sand. Fresh, clean, approachable. Broader appeal, younger audience. Think Cornish Fishmonger.

Bold & Creative

Navy, orange/coral, white. Energetic and distinctive. Stands out from competitors. Think OceanBox.

Dark & Sophisticated

Dark green, cream, black. Restaurant-quality, luxury feel. Premium positioning. Think Wright Brothers.

What Happens Next

The Roadmap

Three phases. Launch first, then grow, then scale.

Phase 1 — Build & Launch

  • 1

    Approve design direction

    Choose Heritage Premium (recommended) or an alternative. Confirm colour palette, fonts, and feel.

  • 2

    Product photography & content

    Steve provides product photos, prices, descriptions. We'll format everything into the site. Real photos are essential.

  • 3

    Build the site

    Homepage, product catalog with Snipcart checkout, delivery info, about page, contact form, recipe section. Full SEO setup.

  • 4

    Connect Stripe & go live

    Steve connects his Stripe account. Test orders. Switch DNS. Go live. Steve manages orders through the Snipcart dashboard.

Phase 2 — Content & Growth 1–2 months post-launch

  • 5

    "Steve's Pick" — Fishmonger's Choice

    Set a budget, Steve picks the best fish. Unique differentiator no competitor can copy.

  • 6

    Seasonal calendar & recipe videos

    What's in season, with buy links. Short phone-filmed prep videos for massive SEO value.

  • 7

    Click & Collect + Gift vouchers

    Order online, pick up from the shop. Digital gift vouchers for birthdays and Christmas.

Phase 3 — Scale 3–6 months

  • 8

    Subscription boxes

    Weekly/fortnightly fish box. Recurring revenue + loyal customer base.

  • 9

    Platter builder & loyalty programme

    Interactive platter builder for events. "Catch Club" loyalty rewards for repeat customers.
